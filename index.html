Nani Ji Ka Ghar

Jab bhi aap nani ke ghar aate the, wo ghar ek mela ban jaata tha. Darwaza khulte hi wo awaaz aati thi — "Aa gayi meri gudiya!" Wo ek feeling thi jo koi word describe nahi kar sakta.

👘

Nani Ji Ke Kapde

Unke purane zamane ke kapde — woh gehri rang ki sarees, woh sunahri kinari, woh khushboo jo sirf unke paas hoti thi. Woh pehnte the toh lagta tha koi raani chal rahi hain.

🍲

Nani Ji Ka Khaana

Unke haath ka khaana — woh dal, woh roti, woh meetha... Chahe kuch bhi banaayen, khushboo aati thi toh pet aur dil dono bhar jaate the. Koi chef match nahi kar sakta.

💬

Nani Ji Ki Baatein

Raat ko unke paas baith ke sunna — woh purani kahaaniyaan, woh hasi, woh "Tu meri beti hai, tujhe koi takleef nahi aane denge" — woh words aaj bhi dil mein gunjaते hain.

Nani Ji Ke Anmol Nuskhe

🌿
Sehat Ka Nuskha "Roz subah ek gilaa paani peena, sab dard door ho jaata hai bete." Unka ye nuskha koi doctor nahi batata tha — par kaam karta tha!
💛
Rishton Ka Nuskha "Zyada mat soch, bas pyaar karo aur maafi maang lo — ghar mein sukh rehta hai." Kitni badi baat thi woh — itni seedhi, itni sachchi.
🌙
Neend Ka Nuskha "Raat ko sone se pehle doodh peena — neend bhi aayegi aur sapne bhi achhe aayenge." Unka woh pyaar bhi ek nuskha tha.
🙏
Zindagi Ka Nuskha "Khush raho, sabse milo, aur Bhagwaan pe bharosa rakho — zindagi mein koi takleef nahi rukegi." Ye nuskha unki poori zindagi ka saar tha.

Nani Ji Ka Aashirvaad

Nani Ji Kehti Hain…

"

मेरी प्यारी बच्चियों —
वर्षा और मनीषा —

"Main jaanti hoon, aaj tum mujhe bahut yaad kar rahi ho. Aankhein bheeg rahi hongi, dil bhaari hoga. Par sunna — main kahin nahi gayi. Main wahi hoon jahan tha — tumhare dil mein, tumhare ghar mein, tumhare har pal mein.

Jab bhi chai ki khushboo aaye — main wahan hoon. Jab bhi raat ko tara chamke — woh mera salaam hai. Jab bhi tumhare chehere par muskaan aaye — woh mera hi aashirvaad hai.

Mera aashirvaad tum dono ke saath hai — Varsha, meri gudiya, aur Manisha, meri beti — humasha, humasha, humasha."

मत रोओ मेरे लिए —
मैं हमेशा तुम्हारे साथ हूँ। 🙏

💛 Varsha 🌸 Manisha
🌸 ✦ 🕯️ ✦ 🌸

Meri Priya Varsha aur Manisha Ji,

Kuch rishte anant hote hain — unhe na waqt khatam kar sakta hai, na doori, na alvida. Nani Ji ka pyaar ek aisi roshni hai jo kabhi bujhti nahi. Unki hasi, unki aawaaz, unka woh haath jab wo siir pe rakhti thin — woh yaadein tumhari umar bhar saath chalenge. Varsha — nani ko tum per bahut fakhr tha. Woh chahti thin ki tum khilkhilao, bado, muskuraao. Unka sapna tha tumhari khushi. Manisha Ji — ek maa ka dil hamesha apni beti ke liye dhakdhakta rehta hai. Aur aaj bhi, kahan se bhi, Nani Ji ka dil aapke liye dua kar raha hai. Rona mat — balki jab bhi Nani Ji ki yaad aaye, ek diya jalao, ek muskaan laao, aur kahna: "Nani Ji, hum theek hain — aur hum hamesha aapko yaad rakheinge."

— Nani Ji ki yaad mein
Pyaar se, 💛

🌸 Varsha aur Manisha — Nani Ji ka aashirvaad hamesha tumhare saath hai 🌸
